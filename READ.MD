## 👋 heyy, it's gourav

i'm **Gourav Roy**, a 3rd year college student who accidentally signed up for a long-term situationship with Data Structures and Algorithms. some days it's love at first `O(log n)`, some days it's `Segmentation Fault (core dumped)` — either way, we keep showing up.

this repo is basically my dsa diary. every problem i solve, every dumb mistake, every "wait it actually works??" moment — it all lives here. no big claims of becoming a competitive programming legend overnight, just tryna get 1% sharper every day and actually understand **why** something works instead of copy-pasting a pattern and hoping.

if you landed on this page — welcome to the grind. pull up a chair, judge my code respectfully 🖤

## 🧠 what's actually happening in this repo

- DSA problems solved in **C++**, sourced from LeetCode / GFG / Codeforces / wherever the chaos is coming from that day
- notes written for future-me, so i stop relearning the same concept every three weeks
- topic-wise folders, because nobody needs 200 files named `sol1.cpp`, `sol_final.cpp`, `sol_actually_final.cpp`
- commit messages with zero filter and occasional emotional damage

## ⚙️ stack

just C++. fast, unforgiving, occasionally makes you question your life choices at 2am. we don't multi-language larp here, we lock in.
